About

This project is designed to enable real-time communication for specially abled individuals using advanced AI techniques. It uses machine learning models to recognize hand gestures and convert them into speech or text, bridging the communication gap efficiently.

Sign Language Recognition
Our system bridges the communication gap for specially-abled individuals through advanced sign language recognition. By utilizing machine learning algorithms, we process uploaded images or videos of hand gestures to predict their meanings accurately. The technology ensures real-time predictions and supports multiple sign language standards, fostering inclusivity and accessibility.

How it Works:
1.Upload: Users can upload images or video clips of sign gestures.
2. Processing: The system analyzes the input using a deep learning model trained on thousands of gesture images.
3.Prediction: Results are displayed in seconds, providing accurate translations of the gestures into text or speech.


Speech Recognition
Our speech recognition feature empowers seamless communication for all. It converts spoken words into text or other outputs using cutting-edge natural language processing (NLP) techniques. Designed to understand diverse accents and dialects, it ensures inclusivity and usability in various scenarios.

How it Works:
1.Voice Input: Users can speak directly into a microphone-enabled device.
2.Analysis: The audio is processed with AI models capable of understanding natural speech patterns.
3.Output: The recognized speech is translated into text or commands for further actions.


Vision and Commitment

We aim to make technology accessible to everyone, fostering a world where communication barriers are eliminated. Whether through visual or auditory inputs, our system ensures inclusivity, enabling specially-abled individuals to interact effortlessly with the world around them.
